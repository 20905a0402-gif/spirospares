
WHAT IS COVERED?
1. TPO Motorcycle Private Insurance (Third Party Only for Private 
Motorcycles)
✅Covers third-party property damage or injury.
✅Legal expenses covered for third-party claims.
✅Includes compensation for third-party deaths or injuries.
❌Does NOT cover the insured’s own damages or injuries.
❌Excludes damage under the influence or deliberate acts.
❌Rider is not covered
2. Comprehensive Motorcycle Private Insurance (Full Coverage)
✅Own damage coverage (accident, theft, fire, natural disaster).
✅Third-party liabilities fully covered.
✅Passenger protection.
✅Legal fees included in claim coverage.
❌Rider is not covered
3. TPO Motorcycle PSV Insurance (For Public Transport)
✅Protects third-party injuries and damages.
✅Covers legal liabilities and accident-related costs.
❌No compensation for the insured’s motorcycle.
❌Claims may be denied for illegal or unsafe operations.
❌ Rider is not covered
4. Comprehensive Motorcycle PSV Insurance (Public Service vehicles)
✅Damage, theft, and fire protection for the insured motorcycle.
✅Full passenger and goods liability coverage.
✅Covers legal expenses and personal injuries.
❌Non-compliance and intentional damage excluded.
❌ Rider is not covered


Key Differences between Private and PSV Coverage
Private Motorcycles: Best for commuting and personal use. Offers flexible options based 
on rider’s risk appetite.
PSV Motorcycles: Designed for public use (e.g., boda bodas), providing extended liability 
and passenger coverage.
