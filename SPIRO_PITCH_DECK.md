### **Feature 2: Rise Request (Back-in-Stock Alerts)**

**The Problem:** Out-of-stock = lost sale

**The Solution:**
1. Rider clicks "Notify Me" on unavailable part
2. TAMTECH captures WhatsApp/phone
3. Auto-notification when stock arrives
4. Rider converts → sale recovered

**Data Bonus:** Demand signals visible in TAMTECH dashboard — informs procurement.

> *"Never lose a sale to stockouts. Rise Request captures demand and auto-notifies riders."*
